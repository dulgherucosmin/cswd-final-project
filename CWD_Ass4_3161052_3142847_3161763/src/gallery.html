Rigby:


Rigby is one of our cats that was adopted a while ago. Her has fainting goat syndrome which causes her tongue to be out all of the time, she found a loving owner that welcome her into their home.

Sam:


Sam was one of our oldest animals at our adoption centre by the time he was welcomed into his new home. He was found when he was a pup and stayed with us for a number of years before being adopted and we hear from him sometimes about his adventures going on walks.

Skittle:


Skittle was adopted a few years after she was found being mistreated at her previous home, she was taken in by us and cared for before she was ready to meet people again when she was adopted into a caring home.

Nora is a German Shepherd who was found as a stray not too far from where we are located and was tested for fleas and worms, and was given her vaccinations before being adopted into a big family with other dogs.
